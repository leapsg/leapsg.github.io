Supporting survivors.

Leap gives survivors of domestic abuse access to legal, medical, and emotional support.

Get Started Why Us?

Discreet and free access to resources.

From medical assistance to legal guidance, Leap connects you with professionals and resources that suite your needs.

Get access to government social services, checklists for taking legal or other action, and other information to support you.

Seek guidance from trained volunteers.

Completely Anonymous

Leap maintains your anonymity as you use the app so you can seek help without comprising your safety.

Trusted Professionals

Leap connects you to real, verified professionals who can help you navigate your personal situation.

Empowering survivors to navigate their circumstances.

Safety Mode

Use the safety mode with a panic gesture that turns the Leap app into a lookalike "health-tracking" app.

Easy Checklists

Access handy lists of documents, medical or legal evidence, personal safety plans, and more.

Secure Evidence

Safely store media and documents such as audio recordings, medical certificates, and more.

Support Group

Get access to a virtual, anonymous support group with other users and volunteers. Use the forums to post questions and seek advice discreetly.

Connect with Resources

Easily and safely access medical, legal, and social services through the app. Leap connects you with trained volunteers and social workers.

Offline Use

Most resources within the app do not require an internet connection to use. Other services, such as forums, require internet connectivity.
